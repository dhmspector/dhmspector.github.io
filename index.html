zeitgeist |ˈtsītˌgīst, ˈzīt-|

What. On. Earth. Is. A. 'Zeitgeist'..? Well, it's anoun.
It's most clearly defined as "the defining spirit or mood of a particular period of history as shown by the ideas and beliefs of the time," for example: "...bell-bottoms, flower-power, Woodstock, anti-war sit-ins, and the heroic struggle for civil rights: the iconic sights, sounds and movements that captured the zeitgeist of the late-1960s."
